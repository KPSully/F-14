In the eleventh video, he talks about masking. Masks are created on layers.
When you are working with a map, white is opaque and black in transparent.
A mask allows you to paint with greyscale values to modify the opacity.
You can copy and paste a mask. You can use a particle brush on masks. You can also you the geometry tool to select faces to create masks.
You can also select UV shells. You can select faces and remove the mask from those faces.
You can also use a mask to help you accurately paint. You can use the I key to invert the mask.
You can also use colors to generate a mask.
