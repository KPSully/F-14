In the sixth video, he discusses the viewport. 3D navigation is akin to Maya's 3D navigation, you use the alt key as a mod key and use mouse buttons to pan, scroll, and rotate.
To rotate you press Alt+Left click.
To pan you use Alt+Middle click.
To zoom, you use the scroll wheel.
Where ever you click on the mesh will be the pivot point.
Shift+Right click to change the lighting.
F will focus on the mesh.
Alt and shift will enable snapping.
M takes you to the material mode.
C runs through all the channels.
